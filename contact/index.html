Contributions

We think your active participation is crucial for this project to fulfil its vision. Your contribution is possible and appreciated in various forms, for example through

  • giving feedback - we always like to hear and discuss your ideas, comments or feedback
  • becoming a partner - if your organisation might be interested in using Influenzanet tools or contributing to the development in any way (legal, ethics, design, research or software development)
  • volunteering - if you are a developer or a designer and looking for an interesting and impactful project to work on
  • backing - if you feel to support the vision of Influenzanet, you can donate to help funding development and hosting

You can directly start or join a discussion on the Github repository or write us an e-mail and we will make sure the right person gets the message.
